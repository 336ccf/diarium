Arfo

Arfo is a stringed musical instrument. Was invented by me at July of 2020.

Creating idea becames from musical instrument – harp. Althought it was recreated in collaboration with guitar-type stringed musical instruments. Netherless never call it as a guitar, it is combination between a guitar and a harp, defined constantly as an arfo.

To create arfo you should have weritified emotions. Some wood and metal strings, if you are not ancient greek hero from cartoons, you should have some instruments to make it real. I have been using drill, hammer, screwdriver, saw, chisel and a grindstone.

Essentially arfo contains 2 parts head and body. There are no limits in size of body and head. Anyone creates in its own image. Sizes definetely will interchange sound of a musical instrument, but it will be always beutiful because it is arfo.

If we are trying to segregate arfo for more than 2 parts, there are only 2 parts. Guitar have defined neck that should be counts as third part of musical instrument, because head that holds strings connect to the neck. Vice versa arfo have no separated neck and it connected to the head directly. Head holds strings across small holes on the top. In difference between guitar and arfo strings do not lie onto fold line between neck and head. Strings from the top directly connect to the bottom with only pair fold points to save a tension of strings.

Length of the head define possible sound controls. Tall head make sound more universal, vice versa short head make sound simplier.

Size of the body define sound volume. Big body would make sound louder to people around, if they even have not heard it; vice versa miniature body should make sound hearable only for a smaller aria around, where even quite sound will be heard by people who is interested in it.

Bottom carrier able to move and change tension of strings. It is newborn feature in stringed musical instruments, that makes it unique. By the way power of tension critically vulnerable on the sound of stringed instruments. Opportunity to change tension of string fastly and simply creates millions of new sounds inside musical composition.

Musical instrument arfo able to edit tension from both sides of an instrument. Top tension should be setted up before song. Bottom tension always in action.

Bottom carrier connected to a side of body in parallel to the head carriers. Bottom carrier is a plank onto which strings are constantly clamped.

Top carriers control each one of string independently on the top. Top carriers is iron eco-friendly fixators. That should be carefully touched and twice more carefully curled.

This instrument have an opportunity to have endless amount of strings, that controls only by the power of player. For example 100 strings will make arfo 100 x 0.5 mm + 5 cm = 55cm in width that makes it absolutely uncomfortable to play. A lot of strings accepted by the reason with harp similarity. When top and bottom connected directly, it losts back-platform dependency to press strings onto.

In first arfo, I was created it 4x string with 0.5 mm distance between strings. P.S. this arfo have capability for second part. Head with 4 strings, but now the placeholder for second head is locked up with wooden plank.
