    Education

    I have an idea to make study free and more powerful. I want to make education process simple in way of study, I want to make study similar to do something. I really like practical part in every study process. I think that theory and practice needs to be together in moment. And this is may be after creating digital education.

    My life over 3 times give me hint that I need to change education, I really like to study and can’t do it with structures. I do it only for me. I need to show for all people how they can study and make benefit.

    I think that all work and thought may be sold for companies that interested in works by scholars. It’s may be course works for themes or referees or etc.

    I think that on practice we know a lot of more than on simple theory but we need to combine it in real moment. To create practice after theory process that show and train all failures.

    I want to make more that simple school, I want make it like culture and study. It’s may be games, online courses, distance communication, offline community classes and more other that may show different material. My target to make people interested in science and study everywhere and anytime. Because if every personality may think like professor or curious man world may be better anyway. I think that this project will be start from game and I make predictions already.
