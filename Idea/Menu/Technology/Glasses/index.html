    Glasses

    Date: April 2019

    I want to use long-term evolution in human anatomy to make something perfect in design. Because there are a lot of different kind of beauty and only smart method of creating may be the best one.

    We had eyes that have round form and these is not something new. People create bulging lens at the moment, but the form and the size was different. I want to make big researching about glasses because it will be our possibilities to understand world better. More than 60 % of all people think that vision is the best method to understand world.
    I want to make something that may be strange and would not accepted by most part of humans. I think that it is not an easy way. I want to make something that may be science motivated in process.
    I see in VR a lot of interesting but at this moment it is bored and worst to health.
    At this moment, I have about 20 different schemes that I need to make in real.
    I could not add some of processing power to these eyes but in all I want to make it in cloud hosting to everyone. Here will be high cost pleasure but it new evolution in technologies.
    I said a lot of water in this sentences, I want to make something constant here. There are may be: Small compactable lenses in ideal, which we cannot do now.
    In additional way, there are may be rounded eyes gags; there are strange seeing from side. Nevertheless, we have possibilities to do something like it really better than other products from market.
    Also there are may be neuron contact to eyes, that describe the power of mind and translate it from eyes, I really cannot say that it will be healthy in start, and have good results. In future we may come to it.

    I think that the simple prototype of these may cost from 100 000 $.

    But in the process of prototyping and creating technical case to manufacturing it might be more over than 5 000 000 $ .
    The time to create something from zero may be equal to 100 – 240 weeks.
